Services

Occlusodontics

Occlusodontics focuses on the different components of the chewing system (teeth, muscles and joints) during rest and function (opening and closing of the mouth,chewing and swallowing).

Implantology

The implant allows you to replace one or more missing teeth. It is an artificial root generally made of titanium, sometimes of zirconia, placed in the dental bone, on which the prosthetic crown will be positioned. The number of implants to be placed depends on the number and location of the teeth to be replaced.

Digital dentistry

At the Casablanca Dental Clinic, we pride ourselves on being constantly up to date with technology in order to provide our patients with the latest developments that dentistry has to offer.We design and manufacture our prostheses by computer when possible. This process is known as CAD/CAM: computer-aided design and manufacturing.
